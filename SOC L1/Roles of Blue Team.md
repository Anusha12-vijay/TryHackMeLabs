## ğŸ¢ Security Operations Center (SOC)

The **SOC** is the first line of defense against cyber attacks.  
It monitors alerts, investigates suspicious activity, and coordinates responses.

### Typical SOC Roles

- **L1 Analyst**
  - Triage alerts
  - Identify false positives
  - Escalate complex cases

- **L2 Analyst**
  - Deep investigation of attacks
  - Malware, phishing, lateral movement analysis

- **SOC Engineer**
  - Configure and maintain SIEM, EDR, and security tools
  - Create detection rules

- **SOC Manager**
  - Oversees SOC operations
  - Coordinates with IT and leadership

SOC is often the **starting point for a cybersecurity career**, providing exposure to real-world attacks.
